Electronic Cognitive Preference Estimator (e-CPE)

One of the key areas of focus within EMPE is that of helping you as an individual and as a team member to become a more effective problem solver. This is aided by having a better understanding of the cognitive processes we all use – and the variations that exist in the way each person uses them.

Electronic Self Assessment of Leadership Behavior (e-SALB)

One of the key areas of focus within EPME is that of helping you as an individual and as a team member to become a more effective leader in differing and challenging environments/situations. The e-SALB instrument was developed to help accomplish this outcome. This self-assessment instrument helps students identify their strengths and areas for improvement based on the Full Range Leadership Development (FRLD) model.

Electronic Conflict Management Style Preference Estimator (e-CMSPE)

Gaining an understanding of which conflict management styles you prefer can help you become an even more effective leader by capitalizing on your strengths and by focusing on development needs, experiences, assignments and training and educational opportunities to help overcome weaknesses.

Electronic Team Members Preferred Role Estimator (e-TMPRE)

Becoming aware of your own team member role preferences, as well as the preferences of other members of a team, can help you: better understand why you prefer a particular role on a team, contribute to the team’s ability to solve Problem A while managing problem B, enhance the overall team performance (i.e. make the sum greater than the whole), and lead teams more effectively.
